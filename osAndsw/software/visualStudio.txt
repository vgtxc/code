// visualStudio自定义安装方法=>命令行安装
	下载vs_installer安装器->安装vs_installer->启动vs_installer用于安装其它组件

参考url:
	https://learn.microsoft.com/zh-cn/visualstudio/install/use-command-line-parameters-to-install-visual-studio?view=vs-2022

<vsSetup.exe> --layout <dstPath>
	<vsSetup.exe>:从官网下载的visualStudio安装器,用于安装vs_installer;
		vs_installer用于安装其它组件
	<dstPath>:vs_installer安装的路径
